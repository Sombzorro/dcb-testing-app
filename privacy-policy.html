Privacy Policy — DCB Test App

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

DCB Test App is an internal test application developed and operated by Boku for the purpose of verifying Direct Carrier Billing (DCB) integrations with Google Play. This app is not intended for use by the general public. Access is not restricted to only authorised testers within Boku and its carrier partners.

Data Collection

DCB Test App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app does not include analytics, advertising SDKs, or any third-party data collection tools.

Purchases

In-app purchases and subscription flows are handled entirely by Google Play Billing. Any payment data is processed by Google and the partner. Boku does not receive or store payment information through this application.

Device Permissions

The app requires the following permission:

  • BILLING — required to initiate Google Play purchase flows for testing purposes

No other device permissions are requested.

Third Parties

The only third-party service used is Google Play Billing, which is governed by Google's own privacy policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

Contact

For questions about this application, contact the Boku integration team at google@boku.com.

Changes

As this is an internal test tool, this policy may be updated without notice. The last updated date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
